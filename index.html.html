New Backpacker's Guide to Hiking and Gear

Are you an aspiring adventurer but are overwhelmed by the amount of gear necessary for an overnight excursion? Most people are, but backpacking does not have to be an uberexpensive and stressful activity. This guide will give you the tips and tricks to buying gear, packing, and planning for your next trip!
